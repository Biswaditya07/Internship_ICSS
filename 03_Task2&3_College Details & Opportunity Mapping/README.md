# College Details & Academic Outreach Mapping | ICSS Internship

## Description

This project focuses on identifying and mapping technical and management colleges in Kolkata for potential cybersecurity training programs, workshops, certifications, industry collaborations, and academic outreach initiatives.

The objective was to create a structured database of institutions that could serve as potential partners for Indian Cyber Security Solutions (ICSS) in expanding its training and educational offerings.

---

## Project Objective

- Identify colleges offering MBA and B.Tech programs.
- Create a centralized database of academic institutions.
- Gather contact details for outreach campaigns.
- Support business development and partnership opportunities.
- Identify potential markets for cybersecurity workshops and training programs.

---
