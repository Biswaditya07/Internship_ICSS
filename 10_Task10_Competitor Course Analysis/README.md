# Competitor Courses vs ICSS Courses Analysis | ICSS Internship

## Description

This project presents a comparative analysis of cybersecurity training institutes and their course offerings against Indian Cyber Security Solutions (ICSS). The study evaluates course portfolios, certification quality, training modes, fees, duration, placement support, strengths, and weaknesses to identify ICSS's competitive position in the cybersecurity education market.

The objective was to benchmark ICSS against leading cybersecurity training providers and identify opportunities for curriculum enhancement, market differentiation, and business growth.

---

## Project Objective

- Compare ICSS courses with major cybersecurity training institutes.
- Analyze certifications, pricing, duration, and training methodologies.
- Evaluate placement assistance and career support.
- Identify strengths and weaknesses of competing institutes.
- Recommend strategies to improve ICSS course offerings and market positioning.
