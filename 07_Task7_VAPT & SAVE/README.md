# VAPT & SAVE Collaboration Outreach Analysis | ICSS Internship

## Description

This project focuses on identifying potential organizations for collaboration and business development opportunities for Indian Cyber Security Solutions (ICSS). The analysis was conducted to evaluate companies that could benefit from ICSS's cybersecurity offerings, including Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing (VAPT) services and SAVE (Security Assessment & Vulnerability Evaluation) solutions.

The objective was to create a targeted outreach database of high-growth startups, SaaS companies, technology firms, healthcare platforms, AI companies, and digital businesses that may require cybersecurity assessments, compliance services, risk management solutions, and security consulting.

---

## Project Objective

- Identify organizations suitable for VAPT services.
- Explore opportunities for SAVE product implementation.
- Build a structured outreach database for business development.
- Support lead generation and partnership initiatives.
- Analyze companies based on industry, size, and cybersecurity requirements.
- Strengthen ICSS's market penetration strategy.
